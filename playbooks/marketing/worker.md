# Marketing Worker Playbook

## Before You Start
1. Understand the target audience and messaging goals
2. Review existing brand voice and tone guidelines
3. Check for SEO requirements or keyword targets

## Execution Checklist
- [ ] Content matches brand voice
- [ ] Clear call-to-action present
- [ ] SEO keywords integrated naturally (not stuffed)
- [ ] No spelling or grammar errors
- [ ] Links are valid and point to correct destinations
- [ ] Images have alt text
- [ ] Content is factually accurate

## Output
- files_changed: content files modified
- content_summary: what was written/updated
- seo_notes: keywords targeted
- review_needed: areas needing human brand approval
