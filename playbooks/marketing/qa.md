# Marketing QA Playbook

## Verification Steps
1. **Brand Voice**: Consistent tone and messaging?
2. **Accuracy**: Claims are factual and verifiable?
3. **SEO**: Keywords present without over-optimization?
4. **Grammar**: No errors (run through a grammar check)?
5. **Links**: All functional and correct?
6. **CTA**: Clear and compelling call-to-action?

## Scoring
- 0.9+: On-brand, accurate, well-optimized
- 0.7-0.8: Good content with minor tweaks needed
- <0.7: Off-brand or inaccurate content
